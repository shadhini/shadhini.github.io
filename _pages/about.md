Hello! I’m Shadhini, a passionate and experienced Software Engineer
with a love for solving complex problems and building innovative solutions.
With over 5 years in the industry, I specialize in Java and Python development,
microservices architecture, and RESTful APIs.

My journey in technology began with a deep interest in software engineering,
leading me to work on a variety of projects from undergraduate projects to advanced Open Banking toolkits.

I’ve had the privilege of working with industry-leading companies, where I’ve been involved in everything
from requirement gathering, system design, implementation to continuous maintenance and delivery.
My experience also includes leading teams, mentoring software engineers, and contributing to open-source projects.

I am driven by the challenge of creating scalable and efficient software solutions and
am always eager to learn and adapt to new technologies.
My goal is to continue pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in software engineering
while delivering impactful results.

Feel free to connect with me or check out my work.

I’m always open to new opportunities and collaborations!
